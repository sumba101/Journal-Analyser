Journal Analyser

Journal Analyser is a webapp that acts as a personal Hindi journal for each user. The application uses latest advancement in Natural Language Processing to track and analyse emotion and distress in user


The goal of the application is to allow people to track their emotional trend across time and act as early warning system against depression
